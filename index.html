Hunt In The DARK (2021)
Hunt In The DARK is the character song for Ignis, released on the Ultraman Trigger: New Generation Tiga Character Song Mini Album. The instrumental version of the song was used in the series multiple times.

Artist: Kei Hosogai
Released: 2021
Album: Ultraman Trigger: New Generation Tiga Character Song Mini Album
